The **2026 General Assembly of the DIAMOND project**, the digital platform of the PEPR DIADEM, will be held on **June 11–12 in Lyon**, on the **La Doua campus**.
It will start on the morning of **Thursday, June 11** with an optional training session on Jupyter Notebooks, and will conclude on **Friday, June 12 at 1:30 pm**, after a closing lunch.
A dinner in the city center is also planned for the evening of June 11.

</big>

</div>

<br/>

<hr/>

## Preliminary Program

### June 11, 2026

- **9:00 – 11:30** — Jupyter Notebooks training *(optional)*
- **12:00 – 13:30** — Welcome lunch
- **13:30 – 15:00** — Two invited talks
- **15:00 – 18:30** — WP progress sessions: WP0 (30 min), WP1 (1h), break (15 min), WP2 (1h), WP3 (45 min)

### June 12, 2026

- **9:00 – 10:45** — Flash presentations by postdocs *(16 × 5 min)*
- **10:45 – 11:15** — Break
- **11:15 – 12:15** — Use cases (Libelul, …)
- **12:15 – 13:30** — Closing lunch
