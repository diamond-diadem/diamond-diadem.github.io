The **2026 General Assembly of the DIAMOND project**, the digital platform of the PEPR DIADEM, will be held on **June 11–12 in Lyon**, on the **La Doua campus**.
It will start on the morning of **Thursday, June 11** with an optional training session on data analysis in materials science, and will conclude on **Friday, June 12 at 1:30 pm**, after a closing lunch.
A dinner in the city center is also planned for the evening of June 11.
